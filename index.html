What is Bubble Tea?

Bubble Tea is traditionally a sweet milk tea drink with chewy pearls originating in Taiwan. It actually has nothing to do with bubbles!


Recently this beverage has garnered international interest and vendors have emerged with unique renditions.


We're creating a Bubble Tea that draws the best qualities and remains unique to the local Nelson community.

Locally Sourced Ingredients

We're supporting the local community by sourcing ingredients locally and making the chewy pearls by hand. All we're taking is the recipe, our ingredients are from down the road.

Environmentally Friendly

All of our packaging is biodegradable. We've said no to single-use disposable plastics and waste that destroys the planet. We want everything to go back to where it came from. The environment is worth more than a great bubble tea.

Fusion of Food & Cultures

Bubble tea originated in Taiwan. It was a product for people who were used to different tastes. We're rethinking the original flavours and their fusion with the tastes of Nelson.
